About Me

I'm Son, a full-stack engineer based in the Boston area with 8+ years of experience shipping software end-to-end. I moved from Viet Nam to Maine in 2013, the only one in my family to make the journey, to study Computer Science and Mathematics at Bowdoin College. Immersing myself in a completely new culture taught me as much as any classroom did.

I have a strong bias toward innovation. I like the messy early stages of a project the most: talking to users, prototyping quickly, and figuring out what actually deserves to be built. I tend to spot gaps before they become problems and build solutions that others end up adopting. What keeps me going is building scalable, high-throughput systems and tools that make users' and developers' lives easier.

Outside of work, you'll find me rock climbing, hiking, biking around Boston, or exploring the city's food scene.
